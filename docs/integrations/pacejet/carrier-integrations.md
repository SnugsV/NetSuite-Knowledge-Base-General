# Carrier Integrations

## Quick Summary

Pacejet integrates with dozens of carriers — small parcel, LTL, freight, and international. Each carrier has specific configuration requirements, service levels, and label formats.

## Difficulty

Intermediate

## Estimated Time

15 minutes

## Carrier Categories

| Category | Examples | Use Case |
|---|---|---|
| **Small Parcel** | FedEx, UPS, Armour, Unishippers | Packages under 150 lbs |
| **LTL (Less Than Truckload)** | Estes, XPO, TForce | Pallets and large freight |
| **Regional** | Day and Ross (Canada), Evans | Regional delivery networks |
| **International** | FedEx International, UPS Worldwide | Cross-border shipping |

## Carrier Configuration

Each carrier requires:

| Configuration | Description |
|---|---|
| **Account credentials** | Carrier account number or contract ID |
| **Service levels** | Which services to offer (ground, express, etc.) |
| **Rate agreement** | Contract rates or published rates |
| **Label format** | Standard or custom label configuration |
| **Surcharges** | Fuel, residential, delivery area surcharges |

## Common Carriers

| Carrier | Type | Special Considerations |
|---|---|---|
| **FedEx** | Small Parcel, International | Multiple service levels, integrated label generation |
| **Armour** | Small Parcel | Regional coverage, competitive rates |
| **Unishippers** | Small Parcel | Third-party logistics provider |
| **WorldWide Express** | Small Parcel | Global shipping solutions |
| **Estes** | LTL | Freight shipping, liftgate requirements |
| **Day and Ross** | LTL (Canada) | Canadian domestic shipping |
| **XPO** | LTL | Freight, final mile |
