# Sales Glossary

## Quick Summary

A reference of terms used in sales and order management in NetSuite.

## A

**Accounts Receivable (AR)**
The department and ledger account responsible for collecting payments from customers.

**Average Order Value (AOV)**
Total revenue divided by the number of orders. A measure of order size.

## B

**Backorder**
An order line that cannot be fulfilled because inventory is not available.

## C

**Credit Hold**
A temporary block on a customer's orders due to exceeding their credit limit or other credit concerns.

**Credit Limit**
The maximum outstanding balance a customer is allowed to carry.

**Credit Memo**
A document that reduces a customer's account balance, typically issued for returns or adjustments.

**Customer Record**
The master data record containing a customer's name, addresses, contact information, terms, and credit information.

## D

**Days Sales Outstanding (DSO)**
The average number of days it takes to collect payment after a sale. Lower DSO is better.

**Drop Ship**
A fulfillment method where items are shipped directly from the vendor to the customer.

## F

**Fill Rate**
The percentage of customer demand that is fulfilled from available stock.

**Fulfillment**
The process of picking, packing, and shipping items to fill a customer order.

## I

**Item Fulfillment**
A transaction recording the shipment of goods to a customer. Decreases inventory and triggers COGS recognition.

**Invoice**
A document requesting payment from a customer for goods or services provided.

## K

**Kit**
A pre-configured bundle of items sold together as a single sellable unit.

## O

**Order-to-Cash (O2C)**
The end-to-end process from receiving a customer order through collecting payment.

## P

**Perfect Order Rate**
The percentage of orders delivered on time, complete, and undamaged.

**Price Level**
A pricing tier that determines the default prices for a customer group.

## R

**Revenue Recognition**
The accounting process of determining when revenue is recorded in the financial statements.

**Return Authorization (RA)**
A document that authorizes the return of goods from a customer and tracks the return process.

## S

**Sales Order**
The core O2C document that records a customer's commitment to purchase goods or services.

**Special Order**
A sales order for an item not normally stocked, which triggers a purchase order to the vendor.
