# Accounting KPIs

## Key Performance Indicators

### Liquidity

| KPI | Calculation | Target |
|---|---|---|
| **Current Ratio** | Current Assets / Current Liabilities | > 1.5 |
| **Quick Ratio** | (Current Assets - Inventory) / Current Liabilities | > 1.0 |

### Efficiency

| KPI | Calculation | Target |
|---|---|---|
| **DSO** | (AR / Revenue) × Days | < 45 days |
| **DPO** | (AP / COGS) × Days | Industry dependent |
| **Inventory Turns** | COGS / Average Inventory | Industry dependent |
| **Cash Conversion Cycle** | DSO + Inventory Days - DPO | Decreasing |

### Profitability

| KPI | Calculation | Target |
|---|---|---|
| **Gross Margin** | Gross Profit / Revenue | Industry dependent |
| **Operating Margin** | Operating Income / Revenue | Industry dependent |
| **Net Margin** | Net Income / Revenue | Industry dependent |
| **Return on Assets** | Net Income / Total Assets | Industry dependent |

### Period Close

| KPI | Calculation | Target |
|---|---|---|
| **Close Time** | Days from period end to final close | < 5 days |
| **Journal Entry Count** | Number of adjusting entries per period | Decreasing |
| **Reconciliation Completion** | % of accounts reconciled by close | 100% |
