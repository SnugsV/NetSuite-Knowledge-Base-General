# What Is Record-to-Report?

## Quick Summary

Record-to-Report (R2R) is the end-to-end process of capturing business transactions, recording them in the general ledger, and producing financial statements. It is how operational activity becomes financial reporting.

## Difficulty

Beginner

## Estimated Time

10 minutes

## Overview

Every business transaction — buying inventory, selling products, paying employees, purchasing equipment — has an accounting impact. R2R is the process that ensures these impacts are correctly recorded, classified, summarized, and reported.

## The R2R Lifecycle

```
Transaction → Subledger → Journal Entry → GL → Trial Balance → Adjustments → Statements → Close
```

## Departments Involved

| Department | Role |
|---|---|
| **Accounts Payable** | Process vendor bills and payments |
| **Accounts Receivable** | Process customer invoices and collections |
| **Inventory Accounting** | Value inventory, calculate COGS |
| **General Ledger** | Journal entries, reconciliations, period close |
| **Financial Reporting** | Financial statements, variance analysis |
| **Internal Audit** | Controls testing, compliance |
| **Management** | Review financial results, make decisions |

## Why R2R Matters

| Reason | Impact |
|---|---|
| **Legal compliance** | Financial statements must be accurate |
| **Tax reporting** | Incorrect accounting leads to tax penalties |
| **Investor confidence** | Accurate reporting builds trust |
| **Management decisions** | Financial data drives strategy |
| **Banking relationships** | Lenders review financial statements |
| **Performance measurement** | Profitability, cash flow, efficiency |

## R2R vs. O2C and P2P

| Process | Focus | Result |
|---|---|---|
| Procure-to-Pay | Buying goods/services | AP, inventory, expense |
| Order-to-Cash | Selling goods/services | AR, revenue, COGS |
| Record-to-Report | Capturing all transactions | Financial statements |
