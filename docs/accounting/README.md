# Accounting

This module covers the Record-to-Report (R2R) lifecycle — how business transactions flow through the ERP into the general ledger and financial statements. Accounting is where every operational module converges.

## The Record-to-Report Flow

```
Business Transaction (PO, SO, Work Order, etc.)
       ↓
Subledger (AP, AR, Inventory, Payroll)
       ↓
Journal Entry
       ↓
General Ledger
       ↓
Trial Balance
       ↓
Adjusting Entries
       ↓
Financial Statements
       ↓
Management Review
       ↓
Period Close
```
