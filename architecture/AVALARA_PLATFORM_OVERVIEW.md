# Avalara Platform Overview

## Purpose
Define Avalara as an ecosystem platform within the NetSuite knowledge model. This document provides a product-neutral foundation for future tax, compliance, and transaction calculation knowledge articles.

## Business Purpose
Avalara is treated in this repository as a tax automation and compliance platform category. Platforms in this category may interact with NetSuite to support tax calculation, address-based tax decisions, exemption handling, tax reporting, and transaction compliance workflows.

## Primary Capabilities
At a high level, platforms in this category may support:

- Sales tax calculation
- Use tax calculation
- Address validation
- Tax jurisdiction determination
- Exemption certificate support
- Transaction tax reporting
- Tax compliance workflows
- Integration with ERP and ecommerce systems

## NetSuite Touchpoints
Avalara-related knowledge may connect to NetSuite through generic touchpoints such as:

- Customer records
- Customer addresses
- Item records
- Sales orders
- Invoices
- Credit memos
- Tax schedules or tax-related fields
- Exemption information
- Integration records or middleware connections

## Primary Records
Common NetSuite records associated with tax automation platform interactions may include:

- Customer
- Item
- Sales Order
- Invoice
- Credit Memo
- Cash Sale
- Return Authorization
- Location

## Public-Safe Boundary
This document should not include company-specific nexus decisions, exemption rules, tax setup, private customer data, internal approval steps, custom fields, workflows, scripts, screenshots, pricing logic, or private operating procedures.
