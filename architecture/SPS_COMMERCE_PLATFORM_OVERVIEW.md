# SPS Commerce Platform Overview

## Purpose
Define SPS Commerce as an ecosystem platform within the NetSuite knowledge model. This document provides a product-neutral foundation for future EDI, trading partner, and supply chain knowledge articles.

## Business Purpose
SPS Commerce is treated in this repository as an EDI and retail supply chain platform category. Platforms in this category may interact with NetSuite to support structured business document exchange between organizations and their trading partners.

## Primary Capabilities
At a high level, platforms in this category may support:

- Electronic Data Interchange
- Trading partner connectivity
- Purchase order exchange
- Order acknowledgment workflows
- Advance shipment notices
- Invoice exchange
- Inventory information exchange
- Shipment status communication
- Data synchronization between business systems

## NetSuite Touchpoints
SPS Commerce-related knowledge may connect to NetSuite through generic touchpoints such as:

- Customer records
- Vendor records
- Item records
- Sales orders
- Purchase orders
- Item fulfillments
- Invoices
- Inventory data
- Shipping and tracking data
- Integration records or middleware connections

## Primary Records
Common NetSuite records associated with EDI and supply chain platform interactions may include:
