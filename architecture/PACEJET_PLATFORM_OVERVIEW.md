# Pacejet Platform Overview

## Purpose
Define Pacejet as an ecosystem platform within the NetSuite knowledge model. This document provides a product-neutral foundation for future shipping and logistics knowledge articles.

## Business Purpose
Pacejet is treated in this repository as a shipping and logistics platform category that may interact with NetSuite to support rating, shipment processing, carrier communication, label generation, and fulfillment visibility.

## Primary Capabilities
At a high level, shipping platforms in this category may support:

- Multi-carrier shipping workflows
- Shipping rate evaluation
- Shipment creation
- Label generation
- Tracking information exchange
- Fulfillment status updates
- Freight and parcel shipping coordination

## NetSuite Touchpoints
Pacejet-related knowledge may connect to NetSuite through generic touchpoints such as:

- Sales orders
- Item fulfillment records
- Customer shipping addresses
- Shipping methods
- Package and shipment data
- Tracking numbers
- Shipping cost information
- Integration records or middleware connections

## Primary Records
Common NetSuite records associated with shipping platform interactions may include:

- Customer
- Item
- Sales Order
- Item Fulfillment
- Invoice
- Shipping Method
- Location

## Public-Safe Boundary
This document should not include company-specific carrier rules, negotiated rates, warehouse procedures, fulfillment logic, custom fields, scripts, workflows, screenshots, or customer-specific shipping processes.
