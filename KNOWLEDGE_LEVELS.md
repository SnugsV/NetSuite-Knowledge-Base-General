# Knowledge Levels

Defines standard learning levels for all documentation.

| Level | Audience | Goal |
|---:|---|---|
| 100 | Beginner | Understand terminology and core concepts. |
| 200 | Power User | Perform day-to-day business processes confidently. |
| 300 | Administrator | Configure, secure, and maintain the platform. |
| 400 | Consultant | Design implementations and troubleshoot complex issues. |
| 500 | Solution Architect | Design cross-functional and cross-platform solutions. |
| 600 | AI Agent | Apply structured reasoning across multiple knowledge domains. |

## Usage

Every article should identify its primary knowledge level.

Articles may optionally reference prerequisite articles from lower levels and advanced articles from higher levels.

## Progression

Learning should flow from foundational concepts to functional knowledge, then technical implementation, solution architecture, and finally AI reasoning.
