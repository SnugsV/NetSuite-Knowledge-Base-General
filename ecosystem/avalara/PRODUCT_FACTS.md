## What Avalara Is
Avalara provides tax compliance software and related services. In the NetSuite ecosystem, Avalara is commonly associated with tax calculation, sales and use tax compliance, exemption certificate management, tax return preparation, filing, and tax-related automation.

## Avalara and NetSuite
Avalara describes its NetSuite offering as a way to automate sales and use tax calculation, returns, exemption management, and related compliance activities from within a NetSuite solution.

Avalara also describes NetSuite connectors that can integrate compliance solutions into NetSuite editions and OneWorld environments.

## Address-Based Tax Calculation
Avalara states that AvaTax uses geolocation and address verification to calculate sales tax down to a specific address.

Avalara also notes that, for the most reliable rates, AvaTax needs an accurate and complete address or latitude and longitude in the United States.
