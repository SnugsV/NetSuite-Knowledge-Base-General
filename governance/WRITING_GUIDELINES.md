# Writing Guidelines

## Write for Learning

The goal is not to rewrite a product manual. The goal is to help someone understand how NetSuite works and how to use it effectively.

## Use Practical Examples

Include practical business scenarios when possible, but keep them generic and public-safe.

Example:

> A company with two warehouses may use an inventory transfer to move stock from the main warehouse to a regional warehouse without changing the total inventory value.

Do not use named companies, private customer examples, internal SOPs, custom fields, saved searches, workflows, SuiteScripts, screenshots, pricing, credentials, proprietary processes, or details that reveal how a specific organization operates.

## Avoid Overloading Articles

If a section becomes too long, split it into its own article.

## Keep Vendor References Separate

Use Oracle references for deeper reading, but keep the main article original.
